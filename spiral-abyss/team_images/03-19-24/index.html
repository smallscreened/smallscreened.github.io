Comments:

Clearing with Diluc last cycle got me wanting to clear with other carries from the standard banner, which is why I ran a Keqing aggravate team this time. It was a little bit of a dicey proposition since I was relying on stats from Harbinger of Dawn while also not bringing a healer, but Kirara did an admirable job keeping her protected. On the other side, I wanted to try and optimize for killing the Wenut. In killing this annoying boss a few times, I've learned that (in addition to memorizing attack patterns), you are best served using characters with short damage windows, low cooldowns, and minimal setup. This is where mono hydro shines, particularly with Ayato as a driver. This is probably the first time I've defeated the Wenut without having to down it twice and I'm definitely going to use this strategy every time it appears in abyss moving forward.
