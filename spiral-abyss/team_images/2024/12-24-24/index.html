Another interesting abyss! I wanted to add another character to the "cleared floor 12 club" so I had Kachina support my Neuvillette team, which was straightfoward enough. The other side was the side where I needed to get more creative. To take advantage of the blessing, have the right tools, *and* be able to survive, I built a strange team with Arlecchino using Faruzan as a support. I settled on Faruzan because Zhongli was needed for the Tulpa (I initially tried clearing with Lyney with no shileder and it went... horribly) and I wanted some good pyro sub dps from Xiangling, but I still needed a bow for the flying boss on 12-2. As an anemo character, Faruzan still can hold VV artifacts so it worked quite well, though it's not exactly the most efficient use of her talents. A win's a win though so I'll take it!
