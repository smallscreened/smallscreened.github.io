Comments:

After getting the idea in my head last cycle, I am now on a mission to clear abyss at least once with each character I have. This cycle I added three new characters to that list with Mona, Sayu, and Yun Jin all being part of my lineup. Clearing with Mona also marks a milestone: I have earned all 36 starts in abyss with every 5-star character I own at least once now. I'm not sure I'll get this ambitious in future cycles, as clearing with this setup this time was pretty difficult (i.e. I absolutely do not recommend these teams). The side objective here was also to continue demonstrating Hu Tao's relevance in the wake of Arlecchino's release. A lot of community members wonder if Father is just a direct powercreep of the Wangsheng Funeral Parlor director, and I'm not sure I see it that way. They both fulfill similar roles, but they almost never want to use the same teammates (though to be clear, they totally can). Perfect example here is how Hu Tao becomes a bit of a nuke dps with Mona and Bennett while Arlecchino can make full use of Yun Jin's normal attack buff in ways that Hu Tao can't. Anyway, hoping that Abyss stays at this point of difficulty so I can continue finding ways to bring more characters through abyss for the first time without having to retry fights 1,000 times.
