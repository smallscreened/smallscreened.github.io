Comments:

I am not too sure how much longer the plunge meta will be particularly well suited to spiral abyss, so I tried out a alternate version of a Diluc team that feels quite a bit more flexible than the Bennett variant. It also just s happened to make both of my teams 5-star only teams, which is a little unusual and novel. Without Furina to support Neuvillette on side one, I did the only sensible thing, which was plug in Childe to act as a burst support. It really didn't felt like he added much, but I guess that's kind of the thing with Neuvillette. He's clearly very strong, but it feels like he has only one real support for him. Everyone else provides some utility, sure, but almost all of it isn't as useful for him as it is for someone else. Anyway, that doesn't really matter because he still tears through everything just fine.
