Comments:

I waited till I got Emilie to go through the abyss, and it resulted in one of my fastest clears yet. To be specific, I have to go back eight months to find an abyss clear that was faster than this one, and this enemy lineup has the largest hp pool of any lineup to date.

To talk teams: You won't see it in the overview stats since my Arlecchino already hits so hard, but having Emilie pile damage onto a team with arguably the two best pyro dps characters while all of them scale off of attack (which Bennett--of course--buffs) really does wonders for this team's ability to shred through basically anything without pyro immunity. As for the other side, I slightly modified my Neuvillette team to add some defensive utility with Beidou and fully leveled a Skyward Pride for her to let her build energy even as a solo electro. It was a lot comfier than the last clear of this lineup, though it's unclear whether this team would perform well enough to clear without the huge cushion that my first side team was able to establish for basically every chamber.
