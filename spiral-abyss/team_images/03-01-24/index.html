Comments:

I love character designs like Xianyun because of how they transform the meta. Diluc has been languishing as a character who basically only brings some raw damage that got outpaced by other characters who work better in his core teams. Now with Xianyun, he brings more raw power in a niche that he is peerless in still: plunging! It's not any easy team to control on mobile, but it hits like a truck and is satisfying when it does click. On the other side, since I was feeling nostalgic using Diluc (and also because I needed a bow), I ran Amber in a Hu Tao vape team. This is one of the few situations where she can also bring some high utility since it's easy to swirl pyro off her ult, Elegy of the End brings some good team buffs, and she can run Noblesse so Xingqiu doesn't have to. Fun team, though not as strong as some of Hu Tao's other options. More than enough for this cycle of abyss, though!
