Deadly Assaults feel like they refresh too fast! Either that or I've just been so preoccupied with building so many characters at once that it feels like I don't have time between challenges to get them all online. That was definitely the case here, as I plan to bench Caesar from Hugo's team for Lycaon just as soon as I have enough resources to actually build him up. Not a lot to comment on in these as I essentially beat the bosses the same way I always do. I'll just say for the record I think Bringer is a bad boss design. I don't necessarily mind enemies that ask you to do specific things to beat them but when the specific thing is a complete pain the ass to do with basically all but one character it's pretty annoying.
