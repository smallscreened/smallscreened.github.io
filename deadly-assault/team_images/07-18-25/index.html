Comments: I don't understand why anyone likes Yixuan. She looks like she's wearing a diaper, has basically zero personality, and her gold damage accents--while cool-looking--are confusing. Of course, there's also the fact that she's the game's latest character pushing the power curve, which I imagine has something to do with it. That said, as someone who will never own Yixuan or Miyabi, I'm pleased to say Deadly Assault is still somewhat manageable. I was worried that the addition of the miasma mechanics and the boss that uses them I'd hit kind of a self-made difficulty wall I couldn't scale, but it turned out to be passable. It's clear my performance is dipping in relation to the rest of the community, though. The summary cards I generate usually report I'm clearing with an average score (around 50%), but this one is 34%, easily my lowest. A win's a win though!
