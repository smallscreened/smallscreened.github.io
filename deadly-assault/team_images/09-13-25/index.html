Comments: Not much to say about this clear, except perhaps that I'd like to wax poetic about the Typhon Destroyer boss. Out of every other big bad enemy in Zenless this one feels like it has a very tangible and satisfying reward for playing around its mechanics. Dodging it accelerates the special stagger you can trigger, and doing so lets you stun it faster, and I'm not sure many other bosses are this clearly designed to give you such a clear reward for playing well. I hope more bosses get designed like this, but I'm pretty sure this guy was in 1.0 and they haven't made anything like it since.
